Quick Answers

Common Questions

What are your support hours?+

Our human support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM IST. AI chat support is available 24/7. For urgent technical issues, we respond within 1 hour.

How long does it take to get a response?+

We typically respond to emails within 2 business hours. Pro and Premium subscribers get priority support with responses within 30 minutes during business hours.

Can I report a technical issue with a course?+

Yes! Use the "Report an Issue" button within any course video, or email us with the course name and lecture number. We resolve most technical issues within 24 hours.

How can I apply to become an instructor?+

We'd love to have you! Fill out this form with "Instructor Application" as the subject, or email us at instructors@eduverse.in. Include your expertise and a sample lesson plan.
