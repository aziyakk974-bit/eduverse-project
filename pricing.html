Pricing FAQs

Can I switch plans anytime?+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.

Is there a free trial for Pro?+

Absolutely! Pro plan comes with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Cancel anytime during the trial.

What payment methods are accepted?+

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI, Net Banking, and wallets (Paytm, PhonePe). All payments are secured by 256-bit SSL.

Do you offer student or group discounts?+

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email get 30% off. For teams of 5+, contact us for custom pricing with team analytics and admin dashboards.
