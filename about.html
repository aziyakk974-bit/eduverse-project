What We Believe

Our Mission & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To make world-class technology education accessible, affordable, and outcome-focused for every student in India. We believe that where you study shouldn't determine where you work.

Every course we build is designed with one goal in mind — to help you get a job, build a product, or level up your skills in the real world.

💡

Our Core Values

  • Quality First — Every course undergoes rigorous review. We'd rather have 10 excellent courses than 100 mediocre ones.
  • Learner Obsessed — Every feature, every update, every decision starts with one question: does this help our learners?
  • Outcome Driven — We measure success by your success — placements, promotions, and projects shipped.
  • Radical Transparency — Honest reviews, real ratings, no fake testimonials. What you see is what you get.

Our Journey

EduVerse Timeline

🗓 January 2022
EduVerse Founded
Started with 10 Java and Web Development courses, 200 early adopters, and a mission to democratize tech education.
🗓 June 2022
10,000 Students Milestone
Crossed 10,000 enrolled students and launched our first live Q&A sessions with instructors. Raised seed funding of ₹2 Cr.
🗓 January 2023
Launched Mobile App
Released iOS and Android apps with offline downloads. Added 200+ courses in ML, AI, DevOps, and Cloud Computing.
🗓 June 2023
Series A — ₹15 Cr Raised
Secured ₹15 Crore Series A funding. Expanded to 500+ courses and launched premium placement support for learners.
🗓 January 2024
50,000 Students & 500+ Instructors
Crossed 50,000 active learners. Onboarded 500+ industry expert instructors and launched AI-powered learning paths.
🗓 2025 — Upcoming
EduVerse 2.0
Launching AI-personalised learning, real-time code playgrounds, 1-on-1 mentorship marketplace, and EduVerse Certifications.
